Compliance & Financial Governance

Nonprofit organizations require strong accountability, regulatory compliance, and financial transparency to maintain donor trust.

  • Development and strengthening of internal financial controls
  • Financial policies and procedures
  • Grant compliance and donor reporting oversight
  • Audit preparation and coordination
  • Financial risk assessment and governance advisory
  • Transparency and accountability reporting frameworks
  • Financial accountability to boards, donors, and regulators

Forensic Auditing & Financial Investigations

When financial irregularities, fraud risks, or compliance concerns arise, organizations need a trusted advisor with the expertise to investigate, document, and resolve them.

  • Forensic financial review and fraud investigation
  • Detection of financial irregularities and misappropriation
  • Recovery of questioned costs and overpayments
  • Coordination with oversight bodies (OIG, auditors, regulators)
  • Strengthening controls to prevent recurrence
  • Financial investigation reporting and documentation
